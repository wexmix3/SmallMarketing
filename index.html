How It Works

  1. The AI assistant greets your customers with a personalized welcome message.
  2. Customers can ask questions about your business, products, or services.
  3. The AI provides instant, accurate responses based on your knowledge base.
  4. For complex inquiries, the AI can collect information and schedule a follow-up with your team.
