Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

How does the AI Customer Service Assistant work?

Our AI Assistant uses natural language processing to understand customer questions and provide accurate answers based on your knowledge base. It learns from interactions over time to improve its responses and can handle multiple inquiries simultaneously.

How long does it take to set up?

Basic setup takes just minutes. Add the embed code to your website, customize the appearance, and you're ready to go. Building a comprehensive knowledge base might take a few hours, depending on the complexity of your business.

Can I customize the appearance to match my brand?

Absolutely! You can customize colors, fonts, themes, logo, and position of the chat widget to match your brand identity. Our Standard and Premium plans offer even more advanced customization options.

What happens when the AI can't answer a question?

When the AI encounters a question it can't answer confidently, it can transfer the conversation to a human agent (on Standard and Premium plans). You can also set up fallback responses and collect customer contact information for follow-up.

Is there a limit to how many conversations I can have?

Our Basic plan includes up to 500 conversations per month, Standard includes up to 2,000, and Premium offers unlimited conversations. If you need more, contact us for custom enterprise solutions.

Can I integrate it with my existing systems?

Yes, our Premium plan includes custom integrations with CRM systems, help desks, e-commerce platforms, and more. We offer pre-built integrations for popular systems like Zendesk, Salesforce, HubSpot, Shopify, and WooCommerce.
